Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India | 11th President of India

About

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India's missile and nuclear programs and was known as the "Missile Man of India".

Achievements

  • Developed India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna (1997)
  • Served as President of India (2002–2007)

Famous Quote

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
